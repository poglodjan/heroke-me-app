I am a young researcher who wants to use my mathematical knowledge to come up with something new. I am currently working on the following three projects:

Research on conscious sleep

I am a member of the start-up Exoself. We are conducting research on conscious sleep and, by interacting with the user at night, trying to enhance their creativity during the day. The start-up is at the level of notes on a piece of paper, has a couple of dozen people from the robotics circle and I am responsible for being an AI specialist. I aim to develop an algorithm to sustain conscious sleep

Auto Machine Learning Framework

I am a member of the circle of mathematical modeling and autoML algorithms at the Faculty of MiNI of Warsaw University of Technology. In the course of the circle's work, we try to build innovative methods combining multiple machine learning algorithms and applying stacking and ensemble learning. My current area is clustering

Applications of neural networks to solve ordinary differential equations

I am conducting a thesis on various methods of approximating ordinary differential solutions through modern neural network approaches. In the course of the work, I am only doing my own implementation of the network, comparing different methods of activation, optimization and architecture. My goal is to confirm that different types of MLPs could be more efficient or faster than traditional numerical methods
